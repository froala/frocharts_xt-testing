Addition of new attribute minLabelWidthPercent for bar charts

In case of a single chart, the maxLabelWidthPercent attribute works as expected. But when the no. of chart is more than one, and we need to align them, then the need of minLabelWidthPercent attribute comes in the picture. As the length of all label may varies across charts. Setting lower value to maxLabelwidthPercent would not be a feasible solution in all scenarios. At this point "minLabelWidthPercent" would help us to achieve the smart label management across charts.
